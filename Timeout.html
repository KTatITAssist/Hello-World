Timeout For Women

 

Who is it for?

Our events are open to all women at all stages of life and from all walks of life:

  • young
  • previously young
  • new Christians
  • long-established Christians
  • women from Greyfriars Church
  • women who go to other churches
  • women who don't go to church at all
In a nutshell, whether you are single, married, divorced, widowed, mothers of babies, grandmothers, women without children. We hope we've made the point that everyone is welcome...as long as you are a woman!





What do we do?

Our main event happens on Thursday morning during school term times.
Where: Greyfriars Church
Time:   9.45 - 11.15am (although the chatting sometimes goes on a bit longer). 
We meet for coffee and cake first.
We then break into small groups for Bible Study or to share our prayer needs and pray for each other.

What does Timout for Women offer?

  • An oasis in the week to meet with other women to study the Bible, pray and generally meet with God, otherwise known as 'fellowship'.
  • A small group setting for those in a home group who also value meeting with other women, or for those who can't or don't want to join a home group.
  • A lifeline for mothers with small children who just want five minutes of peace to themselves to enjoy cake, coffee, chat and time with God.
  • A chance to make new friends and get to know other women at Greyfriars.
  • People to share your troubles and joys with who can offer a wealth of wisdom, sympathy and help.
  • An opportunity for your pre-schoolers to get to know the other children at church.
